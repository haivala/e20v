Mr. Smooth has gone onto becoming one of the most exiting talents to emerge from the electronic music scene in Helsinki. For 15 years he’s been involved in organizing Entropy events, and during the last 10 years he’s also taken turntables by storm. His creative juices have been flowing from click house to dub techno and more banging but equally deep dancefloor techno.
